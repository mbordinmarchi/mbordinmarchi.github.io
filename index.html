About me

I love mathematics. I've spent the last 13 years doing undergraduate studies, a master's degree, and a PhD in math because I enjoy it. It has always been a dream of mine to create a new mathematical result, which I achieved with my doctoral dissertation, and here is the link . (I don't reccomend oppening it)

I am eager to use the logical and analytical knowledge I acquired during these years to solve real business questions or problems. Identifying patterns and analyzing them is at the heart of mathematics, and I also see this as a fundamental aspect of Data Analytics.
